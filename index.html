What is Vooverse?

Vooverse is an upcoming sandbox game supercharged with an ideology of total truth, transparency, and honesty—the one platform you can trust to be at your side. In Vooverse, players, or Voos, will be able to:

  • Collect, sell, and trade Items in a safe virtual economy
  • Create and join Groups of many kinds
  • Connect and interact with fellow Voos
  • Explore and build Worlds
  • Customize their Voos with Items such as Hats and Skins
  • Lock in a membership for next-level benefits
